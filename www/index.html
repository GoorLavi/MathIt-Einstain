how to play

all you need to do is calculate your way to the "a you need" number, by using the calculator buttons. you can see how much pressing left near the "moves left" . any pressing on numbers count one move.

buttons: + , -, x , / count one move either .

buttons : x^ and x^2 count as tow.

button "." isent count at all.

if u calculat close as 98% it will be count as u succeed.

the progress bar will show how much you are close to the number in percents.

if your out of moves you can use try again button and lose one life.

in case some moves left after you arrived to the wanted number , you will receive 2 more lifes.
